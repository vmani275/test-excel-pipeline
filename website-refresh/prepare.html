Prepare Your Household

Atherton residents should be ready to be self-sufficient for at least seven days after a major disaster.

Step 1: Make a Plan

  • Out-of-area emergency contact
  • Household reunification plan
  • Evacuation routes and meeting points

Step 2: Build Supplies

  • Water, food, medications, first aid
  • Battery backup, flashlights, radio
  • Go-bags for each household member

Step 3: Stay Informed

  • Sign up for SMC Alert
  • Know your Area Coordinator and EAP
  • Join monthly A.D.A.P.T. meetings
Preparedness is strongest when practiced together.
