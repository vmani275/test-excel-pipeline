Respond Safely During a Disaster

Use checklists to stabilize your household first, then coordinate with neighbors and your Emergency Assembly Point (EAP).

Immediate Priorities

  1. Protect life and treat urgent injuries.
  2. Check hazards (gas, fire, structural damage).
  3. Listen for official alerts and instructions.

Neighborhood Response

  • Know your Area number and coordinator.
  • Report critical needs through neighborhood channels.
  • Support vulnerable residents when safe.

Training

Take CERT training and participate in drills to build readiness and confidence.

Know your role before an emergency happens.
