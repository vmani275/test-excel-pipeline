About A.D.A.P.T.

Formed in 1999, A.D.A.P.T. is an all-volunteer organization partnering with Atherton public safety and town leadership to strengthen local disaster readiness.

What We Do

  • Help households and neighborhoods build emergency plans.
  • Lead training exercises and preparedness drills.
  • Share trusted materials and communication pathways.
  • Coordinate with police, schools, and area leaders.

Community Leadership

Our board and committees focus on operations, outreach, community meetings, and area-level preparedness support.
