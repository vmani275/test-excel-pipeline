Community Events & Training

A.D.A.P.T. meetings are generally held on the first Saturday of each month at 9:00 AM.

Monthly Meetings

  • Updates from Atherton Police and A.D.A.P.T.
  • Preparedness training segment
  • Resident Q&A and networking

Location

Town Council Chambers
80 Fair Oaks Lane, Atherton

CERT Training

A.D.A.P.T. encourages residents to enroll in Menlo Fire CERT training programs.

Meetings are free and open to residents and neighbors.
